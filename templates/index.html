📖 What is GDP & GDP Growth Rate?

GDP (Gross Domestic Product) is the total monetary value of all goods and services produced within a country in a specific period. It is the most widely used measure of a country's economic size and health.

GDP Growth Rate measures how fast a country's economy is expanding or shrinking compared to the previous year. A positive value means the economy grew, while a negative value means it contracted (recession).

GDP Growth Rate (%) = ((GDP this year − GDP last year) / GDP last year) × 100
